**Purpose of Guarantee:**
To indemnify the Beneficiary (Carrier) against all consequences, liabilities, losses, damages, and expenses that may arise from delivering the goods to the Applicant without the presentation of the original Bill of Lading, which is currently delayed.

**Conditions for Demand:**
1. A written demand for payment, signed by an authorized representative of the Beneficiary.
2. A statement in the demand confirming that the Beneficiary has suffered a loss due to the release of the goods to the Applicant under the specified Bill of Lading, and that the original Bill of Lading has not yet been surrendered to the Beneficiary.
3. The demand must be presented at the counter of the Guarantor on or before the Expiry Date.

**Undertaking:**
The Applicant and the Guarantor jointly and severally undertake to surrender the original Bill of Lading No. B/L-2025-050 to the Beneficiary as soon as it comes into their possession.
