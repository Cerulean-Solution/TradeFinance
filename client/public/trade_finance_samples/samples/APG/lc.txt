1. A written demand for payment, signed by an authorized representative of the Beneficiary.

3. The demand must be presented at the counter of the Guarantor or the Advising Bank on or before the Expiry Date.

**Reduction Clause:**
The guaranteed amount shall be automatically reduced pro-rata by the value of each shipment/delivery of goods certified by the Beneficiary, provided such certification is received by the Guarantor.
