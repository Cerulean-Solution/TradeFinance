**BANK GUARANTEE**

**Guarantee Number:** BG-2024-001235
**Issue Date:** February 01, 2024
**Expiry Date:** January 31, 2025

**Applicant (Guarantor's Customer):**
XYZ Construction Group
456 Industrial Road
Shanghai, 200000, China

**Beneficiary:**
ABC Project Development Corp.
123 Business Street
New York, NY 10001, USA

**Guarantor (Issuing Bank):**
China International Bank
321 Commerce Boulevard
Shanghai, 200001, China

**Advising Bank:**
Global Trade Bank
789 Financial Avenue
New York, NY 10005, USA

**Amount:** USD 1,000,000.00 (One Million US Dollars Only)

**Underlying Transaction:**
Contract for the construction of the New York Office Tower, Contract No. NYC-2024-001, dated January 20, 2024.

**Purpose of Guarantee:**
To guarantee the Applicant's obligation to pay the Beneficiary upon presentation of a written demand stating that the Applicant has failed to perform its payment obligations under the Underlying Transaction.

**Conditions for Demand:**
1. A written demand for payment, signed by an authorized representative of the Beneficiary.
2. A statement in the demand confirming that the Applicant is in breach of its payment obligations under Contract No. NYC-2024-001.
3. The demand must be presented at the counter of the Guarantor or the Advising Bank on or before the Expiry Date.

**Governing Law and Jurisdiction:**
This Guarantee is subject to the **Uniform Rules for Demand Guarantees (URDG), ICC Publication No. 758**.
